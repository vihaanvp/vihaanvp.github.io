Home Farmer
(Active)

Time Built: First prototype in April 2024; Latest prototype in September 2024;

Why I built this:

  • Everyone gets fresh, healthy food in the comfort of their home.
  • The Project matches the theme of Earth Allies (WRO 2024).
  • The robot is useful to the community and the environment, both at the same time!

What I learnt from this: (Don't worry if you don't know all this, I was also there once)

  • How to use stepper motors.
  • How to use a CNC Shield V3 to control stepper motors with a DRV8825.
  • What a Rotary Encoder is.
  • Introduction to GRBL.
  • How to combine all of this to make a working, semi-automatic robot.

My Experience: I learnt how to do all of the things listed above, while working with my teammate to make the Home Farmer a reality! (Though we did not get selected for the National Round, we still got a chance at the Regional and I think we killed it!)

Line Follower Robot
(Completed)

Time Built: Once in 2024 and again in 2025.

Why I built this: For the Annual Techradiance Line Follower Championship (8th and 9th Edition)

Tech Used: Arduino Uno, IR Sensors, Battery (12V), Motors, Motor Driver (L293D), and more minor components

My Experience (First Time): I did a lot of testing, but I didn't consider the possibility that the track would be under a bright light and would be very reflective.

My Experience (Second Time): This time I tested with the scenario of light in consideration, using special IR sensors, but it went off-course anyway because of an air bubble that affected the IR sensors.
